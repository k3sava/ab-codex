# Why Adam Renklint Raised $137M... and Gave it Back

Founding Journey podcast episode (Michael Houck) with Adam Renklint, co-founder and CEO of Pitch.com. The episode covers Pitch's path from $137M raised, an $85M Series B, the realization that the business was not a venture-scale opportunity, the return of capital, and the pivot to a smaller more sustainable model.

Episode topics (from the show notes):
- Starting Pitch with 8 co-founders
- Product-led CEO: pros and cons
- Transitioning from CTO to CEO
- Keeping the team motivated during chaos
- Balancing mourning after layoffs with future focus
- Product usability as a differentiator
- Shipping quickly within a framework
- Changing the aspirations of Pitch
- Discussing aspirational change with investors
- Common pitch deck mistakes
