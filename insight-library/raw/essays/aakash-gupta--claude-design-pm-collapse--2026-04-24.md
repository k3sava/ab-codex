# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-30, 2026-05-01)

Aakash Gupta on the PM-to-design handoff collapsing the way PM-to-engineer collapsed two years ago.

> "[Claude Design is] the tool most PMs will be using in 6 months."

The implicit warning: PMs who keep outsourcing their first artifact will compete with PMs who arrive at design review with a working prototype. AI-native workflow: every PRD ships with a Claude Design (or equivalent) prototype attached, and the designer's first job is critique, not creation. Human checkpoint: design lead approves the visual system before any user sees the prototype.

Companion essay (https://www.news.aakashg.com/p/claude-automation-pms): seven Claude-driven PM workflows (morning briefings, doc syncs, meeting prep, on-call), plus a public GitHub repo. Frame: split a PM's intelligence into a personal layer and a team layer; the PM who builds the personal layer first owns the room.
