# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-27)

Tomasz Tunguz argues Anthropic is running Google's commoditize-the-complement playbook:

> "A free, good-enough product is enough to change market dynamics."

The implication: incumbents and challengers should tag features quarterly as core or complement. Complement features should be conceded gracefully or bundled, not protected; defending complements wastes capital that should fund the core.
