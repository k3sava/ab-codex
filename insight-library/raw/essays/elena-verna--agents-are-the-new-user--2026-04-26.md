# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-27, 2026-04-29)

Elena Verna argues AI agents speaking MCP are now first-class product users:

> "Agents don't care about onboarding. They don't care about your navigation. They care about whether your product returns the right output, quickly, reliably, and in a format they can use."

> "If an agent can reproduce your core functionality without ever touching your product, what exactly are you defending?"

Companion piece, co-written with Gemma Casey-Swift (https://www.elenaverna.com/p/whos-got-time-for-product-marketing): PMM as infrastructure, not gate.

> "PMM stops being the team you wait on and starts being the team that makes everyone else faster."
