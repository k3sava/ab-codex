# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-27)

Glenn Gabe documents a site that scaled AI-generated content to climb Google rankings, then collapsed across organic, AI Overviews, AI Mode, and ChatGPT citations once Google issued a manual action.

> "If you receive a manual action from Google, you will drop in AIOs, AI Mode, and then downstream in AI search."

The risk pattern: manual actions and quality-driven demotions in classic SERP propagate to AI surfaces. The early signal is surge-then-decay shapes in GSC plus citation-log volatility.
