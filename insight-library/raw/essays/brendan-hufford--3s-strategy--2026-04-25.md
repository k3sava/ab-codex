# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-27)

Brendan Hufford reframes where SEO/AEO content actually comes from: not keyword tools, but Sales, Success, and Support.

> "SEO tools are at best 6 to 12 months behind reality."

The 3S model: ingest sales call transcripts, customer-success threads, and support ticket text weekly. Cluster recurring buyer questions. Rank candidate content briefs by frequency × deal size. The gap between what reps are hearing this week and what shows up in search-volume tooling is exactly where the best-converting content lives.
