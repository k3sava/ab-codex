# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-27, 2026-04-28, 2026-04-30)

Chris Orlob (pclub.io) on the collapse of cold outbound:

> "Cold outbound is collapsing. Not declining. Collapsing."

> "If you don't signal insight immediately, you're invisible."

The fix: a sharp, durable point of view per account, earned from public signals (10-K, hiring patterns, product changes), that reframes the buyer's problem and proves the rep already understands their world. Teams running tight-list POV outbound report 3x pipeline vs generic cold outbound, and reply rates lifting from the 1-2% baseline to triple-digit improvements over baseline.

Companion: Sales Competency Analytics piece (https://www.pclub.io/blog/sales-competency-analytics, 2026-04-17). Quoting Rohit Shah:

> "Revenue rarely improves from generic sales training, it improves when training targets the exact decision friction blocking deals."
