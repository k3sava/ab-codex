# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-28)

Andrew Chen names the bottleneck most growth teams hit in 2026: not creative, not media buyers, but the data-plumbing person — the architect who makes the data foundation legible across attribution, experimentation, and AI tooling.

> "Finding the person who can architect the data foundation underneath, the 'plumber,' is excruciatingly difficult."

The growth-team hiring shape reorders: the plumber/data-architect is the highest-leverage early hire, ahead of incremental creative or media-buyer headcount.
