# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-27, 2026-04-28)

Maja Voje and Kyle Poyar surveyed 200 GTM operators in early 2026:

- 67% reported Claude enabled them to do something previously impossible.
- 27% had swapped out an existing tool.

> "The top GTM AI tool stack in 2026 is Claude + CRM + orchestration. These tools are not competing. They are combining."

Implication: AI tooling is not yet a tool-replacement story for most GTM teams; it is a workflow-unlock story that compounds with existing systems of record. The orchestration layer (between AI and CRM) is the role most teams under-resource.
