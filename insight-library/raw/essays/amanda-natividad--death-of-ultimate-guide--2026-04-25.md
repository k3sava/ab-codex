# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-29)

Amanda Natividad argues the "ultimate guide" content play loses its mechanic when an LLM summarizes for the user.

The mechanism: ultimate guides won pre-AI because they out-completed competitors on coverage, anchoring keyword rankings on comprehensiveness. When an LLM is the intermediary, the user gets the summary directly; the guide-writer's coverage becomes invisible feedstock. POV content (sharper, opinionated, perspectival) is retrieved and named because the perspective is what the model can't generate from coverage alone.

Closes the AEO content-side triangle alongside Indig (ghost citation) and King (passage-level relevance engineering): the unit shifts from page to passage, and within the passage, opinion beats exhaustiveness.
