# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-29)

Andy Raskin's April 2026 update repeats the Ben Horowitz line and grounds it with proof points:

> "The company story is the company strategy."

Cited examples: Amplitude, Clearbit, Auvik. Operating move: a monthly narrative-drift audit comparing sales decks, homepage, and product release notes against the canonical story. Resolves contradictions before they reach a buyer; the same canonical story has to hold across all three surfaces or downstream content rebuilds on sand.
