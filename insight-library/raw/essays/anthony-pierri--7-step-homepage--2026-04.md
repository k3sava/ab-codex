Anthony Pierri's 7-step homepage framework (FletchPMM):

Most B2B homepages lead with vision. The buyer needs use case, alternative, and result in five seconds. Lead with the capability — what someone actually does with the product — not abstract benefits or vision language.

Companion: Growth Unhinged "definitive product positioning part one" (https://www.growthunhinged.com/p/product-positioning-part-one) — target customer selection is the load-bearing first move in positioning, before category, before differentiators. Most founders refuse to go narrower than their TAM deck wants.
