# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-05-01)

Chris Orlob (pclub.io) reframes sales coaching around skill-level competency data instead of activity dashboards. Body quotes Rohit Shah:

> "Revenue rarely improves from generic sales training, it improves when training targets the exact decision friction blocking deals."

Mechanism: every recorded buyer call is scored against a fixed competency rubric (discovery quality, multi-threading, objection handling, next-step specificity), and a personalized coaching plan routes to each rep's manager. The shift is from activity metrics (calls per day) to skill-friction metrics (the exact decision the rep is failing to advance).
