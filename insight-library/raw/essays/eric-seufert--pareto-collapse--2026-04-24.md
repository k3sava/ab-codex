# Excerpt (from miniu digest 2026-04-27, 2026-04-30)

Eric Seufert reframes mobile UA in the AI era:

> "AI does not extinguish economic ambition through abundance. It multiplies ambition by making specificity profitable."

Mechanism: AI lowers the cost of producing variants targeting sub-segments, so creative-testing tooling that ranks against incrementality from day one (not after launch) pays disproportionately. The base of the Pareto distribution flattens because more long-tail segments are now profitable to address.

Companion (Q1 Meta breakdown, https://mobiledevmemo.com/meta-q1-2026-earnings-33-ad-revenue-growth-mcp-support-for-ad-buying-agents/): Meta now exposes MCP support for ad-buying agents. Ad revenue +33% YoY, capex guides to $125-145B. The human UA manager's job collapses into prompt design and guardrails.
