# Andrej Karpathy

## Bio
AI researcher and educator. Founding member of OpenAI, ran computer vision and Autopilot AI at Tesla, now building Eureka Labs (AI-native education). Widely-read writer on neural networks, transformers, and the operating practices of working with frontier models. The "LLM Wiki" gist (April 2026) introduced the persistent-knowledge pattern many operator workflows have since adopted.

## Operating themes
- **Persistent knowledge over re-derivation.** Maintain a wiki the LLM owns and edits; do not re-RAG the same questions across sessions.
- **The IDE / programmer / codebase frame.** Obsidian (or any plain-text editor) is the IDE; the LLM is the programmer; the wiki is the codebase. The substrate is plain markdown.
- **Three-layer architecture.** Immutable raw sources, LLM-owned synthesis, small constitution.
