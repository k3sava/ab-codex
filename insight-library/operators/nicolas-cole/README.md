Nicolas Cole. Writer, ghostwriter, and growth educator focused on AI-native content creation and audience building. Publishes Write With AI, a newsletter on using language models to build and distribute content at scale. Known for practical frameworks on digital writing, VOC-driven copy, and converting reader attention into business outcomes without relying on ad spend.

## Operating themes
- **VOC as source material** Customer language found in reviews and forums is the highest-quality input for conversion copy
- **LLM as assembly layer** AI's role in copywriting is pattern recognition and assembly from VOC data, not original authorship
- **Editorial checkpoint** Human review tests whether assembled output sounds like the customer or like an AI approximation of one
