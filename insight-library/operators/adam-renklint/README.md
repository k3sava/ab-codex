# Adam Renklint

## Bio
Co-founder and CEO of Pitch.com — collaborative presentation software for modern teams, founded 2018, launched publicly in October 2020. Started as Pitch's CTO and transitioned to CEO in 2024. Previously at Wunderlist and Microsoft (where he led the cross-platform team behind the task-suggestion algorithm that powered Microsoft To-Do). Notable for raising ~$137M (including an $85M Series B) and then returning capital after the team concluded the business was not a venture-scale opportunity, pivoting to a sustainable model.

## Operating themes
- **Product-led CEO** — transitioned from CTO to CEO with usability as the differentiator.
- **Returning capital as a strategic move** — willingness to give back $137M when the venture math no longer fits.
- **Eight-co-founder team dynamics** — Pitch was founded by eight co-founders, an unusual count Renklint discusses publicly.
