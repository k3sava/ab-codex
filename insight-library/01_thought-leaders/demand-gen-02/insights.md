# Elena Verna Insights

## Insight: Build growth loops, not campaign bursts
- Leader: Elena Verna
- Domain: Demand Gen / Growth
- Maturity: Applied
- Confidence: Medium

### Claim
Sustainable growth comes from loops with explicit inputs, conversion points, and reinvestment logic—not isolated campaigns.

### Why it matters
This gives your Flywheel layer a structural model for prediction, attribution, and reinvestment decisions.

### Mechanism
Map each loop: trigger -> action -> value -> sharing/expansion -> reinvestment; then instrument leading metrics at each step.

### Apply when
- Pipeline is volatile quarter to quarter.
- Team learns slowly from experiments.

### Evidence signals to track
- Loop throughput and time-to-signal.
- Cost per validated insight.
- Revenue contribution by loop cohort.

### OS mapping
- Commons: Loop canvases + experiment logs.
- Flywheel: Cohort loop scoreboard.
- clearPMM: Growth loop operating playbook.
