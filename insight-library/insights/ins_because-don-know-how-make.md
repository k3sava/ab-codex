# Because I don’t know how to make them see I fit

## Claim
“Rewrite my resume to match this job post.” Because I don’t know how to make them see I fit. Because no one reads the story behind the bullet points. Because if I tell the truth, I disappear. Because there’s no checkbox for “didn’t fall apart.” “Compress 12 years of work into 2 pages.” Even though I’m scared, tired, and still trying.
